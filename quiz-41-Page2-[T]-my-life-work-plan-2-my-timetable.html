The final box above is or should be within a couple of months of the date for completing whatever the final stage of your training is. So, if you are going after vocational or technical training, your date to be working in your career should be within a couple of months of completing your vocational or technical training program.

Your timeline will give you a good sense of where you are in implementing your Life-Work Plan

Once you’ve completed the timeline above, you can move on if you choose. This is a step you will also want to share with your mentor team to get their feedback and make sure your timeline is realistic based on your Life-Work Plan. When you click the SAVE button below your answers will create a PDF document titled My Timeline and it will be in your completed documents tab on your dashboard. You can share it from there with your mentor team for feedback.

If you want to create a new timeline after you have saved one, simply reopen this document and edit it with the new plan dates. This time when you click the SAVE button it will create a new Timeline document replacing the previous one. There is no real need to have multiple timelines. You need one good timeline that reflects your current plan. The capacity to create new timelines will make this useful beyond the completion of this course. Plans and timelines change and there is nothing wrong with the need to update a timeline.
