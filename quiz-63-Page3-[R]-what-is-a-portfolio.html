What is a Portfolio?

What does a portfolio look like?

A portfolio is anything that will let you hold, organize, and present a collection of documents and photographs. Through this course you will create a portfolio that will live digitally online and can be shared by email and that can be printed out and organized in a binder or presentation book. This physical portfolio is the portfolio you will take with you to interviews. We recommend something like this.

This is a presentation book with 24 drop-in clear plastic pages giving you room for 48 pages of evidence. They are about $7.00 and are available at most office supply stores like Staples or OfficeMax/Depot. You can also find good options at art supply stores. Book stores like Barnes and Noble often have fancier presentation books with leather covers if you want to up your game, but the basic version described above is a great place to start.
