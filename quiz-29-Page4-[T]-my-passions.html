My Passions

There are six “Passion” blocks below. You may have more passions than that, but for now, let’s focus on the six activities you are most passionate about. If you don't have six passions at this point, complete as many as you can. But you will need to identify at least three passions before moving on. There are two steps to completing each passion block and this task:

  1. Write out each passion in a complete sentence. For example: “I Iove playing role-playing video games,” or “I am passionate about listening to and making music—especially writing lyrics.”
  2. What could you offer as proof of the passion you are claiming? Preferably this would be something that could fit into your personal presentation portfolio. As with your skills inventory, awards and certificates are always nice. If you’ve gone through some kind of special training program, there is usually some kind of document that attests to your participation and completion. Most often, however, photographs of you engaged in that activity or of the places you’ve explored or the things you’ve collected will also work as evidence.

We’re hoping that identifying passions is easier. We certainly hope that you can identify things that you love to do and that bring you joy. Remember that it is our passions that tend to make us interesting and attractive to others. For now, at least three passions listed above will allow you to move on and you can always come back to this task to add passions that you think of and update your “My Passions” document.
