Sample Letter of Recommendation

From: (Your name will be here)

Dear (Your potential Letter Writer’s name will be here)

I am building a resume and professional presentation portfolio as part of a class in Life- Work Skills. As someone who knows me and can attest to at least certain aspects of my character, I am asking you to write me a simple letter of recommendation

Ideally this letter of recommendation would be on stationery or letterhead but plain paper would be fine. It can be addressed “To Whom it May Concern” or left open, as it is going in my portfolio rather than being sent to a particular employer. It is also not for a specific job, so it is fine if you just choose to reflect on the positive aspects of my char¬acter as you have experienced them. What would be most useful is your comment on any aspects of my character that would recommend me to an employer:

  • Reliability and dependability
  • Integrity and honesty
  • Work ethic
  • Communication and presentation skills
  • Intelligence and the capacity to learn new things quickly
  • Positive attitude
  • Conflict resolution skills
  • Ability to work well in a team environment
  • Any unique or special skills

If you have not observed a particular quality, do not feel compelled to address it. Thank you in advance for considering helping me this way.

Sincerely,

(you’ll sign and date before delivering)

PS. I will check back with you in a week unless you would like to give me a specific date to pick up the letter.

Remember that teachers and the people who might write you letters of recommendation are often busy. Give them a week to write it or ask them for a pick-up date. Be polite. Remember that if you schedule a pick-up date that it is important that you follow through and show up to pick it up (and say thank you).
