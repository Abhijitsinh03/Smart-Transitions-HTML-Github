My Gifts Inventory

Having a natural talent for something is sometimes called having a gift. A gift or talent is something that seems to come easy to us. A gift or talent seems to come naturally even without training or practice. We can of course take a natural talent and refine it as a skill. A natural athletic ability might be refined as a skill at playing basketball or baseball or surfing or tennis, for instance, but the talent or gift is athleticism. A good way of knowing whether you have a talent is whether you have ever been complimented or praised for doing this.

Select the 10 talents that you think you possess by checking the box beside it. This exercise will only allow you to select 10 so you will have to choose carefully. If you have chosen 10 and discover something that you think is more truly a gift or talent for you, go back and uncheck one of the other boxes to make room, Take your time with this exercise.
