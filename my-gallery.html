My Gallery

What is my gallery?

The ‘My Gallery’ section is where you can upload all of the documents that you think will be helpful to you as you work through the Smart Transitions program. You can upload files from your phone or computer. As you make progress in the program, you’ll see some documents that you have created through completed tasks appear here. With the exception of your resume, you can decide whether you want to make these documents visible to your mentor team or through a public link in the “My Public Brand” section. If you have a hard copy of a document (like a certificate earned) and want to include it here, you can download a scanning app to your phone and create a digital copy to upload here.
