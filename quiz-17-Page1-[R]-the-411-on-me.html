The 411 On Me

Part of moving into the adult world, especially into the world of work, requires you to have certain information readily available. You will need this information for almost every job application you fill out. You will need this information to create a resume and a professional appearance for yourself. You will need some of this information for college and technical or skills training schools. You will need some of this information when you want to get a license, a state issued photo ID, apply for insurance or health care coverage. Instead of scrambling to gather the information every time you need it, we’ve created an Autobiographical Data Form for you.

Your next task will be to complete your Autobiographical Data Form. If you take the time to fill this out completely and correctly now, it will benefit you in a couple of ways.

  1. It will store all your key information on a cloud-based server that you can access from your phone or mobile device at any time. If you find yourself filling out a job application you hadn't planned on filling out, your data is as close as your phone. Having access to that data and being able to fill out the complete application without blank spaces will make you appear more professional and prepared.
  2. Once you’ve completed your Autobiographical Date Form, creating a professional resume is as simple as pushing a button. Your information will be exported to a professionally formatted resume that you can download and print out or email.
  3. As you move from high school to college or post-secondary skills training, you will gain new educational credentials, certifications and work experiences. Editing and updating your resume is as easy as returning to your Autobiographical Data Form. You simply open it up, delete info that is incorrect or no longer relevant, add educational credentials, certifications, new work experiences, and new skills and then push the Create Resume button.

The information you provide is secure. We do not sell your information or share any information that in any way identifies a particular person. You can check out our official privacy policy here.

The Autobiographical Data Form does take some time to complete. If you can't do it all at once, take a break and come back to it later. You may need to do a little internet research to gather things like the addresses of places you’ve worked. But, if you do this now, it will save you so much time later on. It is so much easier to edit a resume you have already started than to start from scratch.
