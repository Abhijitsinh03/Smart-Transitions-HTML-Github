2nd Secret of a Successful Interview

2nd Secret of a Successful Interview

Energetic Attitude

The single reason most employers give for failing to hire someone is that they don’t seem to have a positive energetic attitude. That translates to an employer as someone who doesn’t really want the job. An interview is absolutely the time for you to bring your “A-game” in terms of energy. You need to show that you want this job and that you’d be good at this job. If neither of those things is true for you, if you don't believe it, then stop wasting people’s time by interviewing for jobs you don’t really want. Before the interview you need to talk to yourself in positives rather than negatives. I deserve this job. I’d be good at this job. Even if you have applied for jobs and not been hired in the past, each interview is a new possibility.

Identify the following statements as true or false based on the reading above:

If you are well-qualified, it doesn't matter how you look at an interview.

Leaning back in a chair tends to make you look too relaxed or even lazy.

If an employer can’t hear you or understand you, that’s their problem.

Good eye contact makes you seem trustworthy and honest.

A lot of people sabotage their chances of getting hired by not seeming like they really want the job.

If you smile at an interview, they will think you are not serious and won't hire you.
