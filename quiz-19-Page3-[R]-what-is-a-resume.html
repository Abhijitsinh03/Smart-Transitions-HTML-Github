What is a resume?

How do I use my Resume?

If you completed enough of your Autobiographical Data Form and clicked “Save My Resume” you should be able to find it under the tab on your dashboard. This is a very clean and simple resume that will work for most of your needs. Once your mentors have approved it, you can publish it. That means you can use it in the following ways.

  1. Download it and save it to your desktop as a PDF
  2. Print it out to include in your physical portfolio
  3. Print out extra copies to take with you to job interviews or to attach to printed employment applications
  4. Attach it to emails when inquiring about employment opportunities
  5. Provide it electronically when asked.

To complete this reading, answer the question below (and yes, you can scroll back and look for the right answer if you need to).

Identify the following statements as true or false based on the reading above:

A resume should be 3 or 4 pages in length.

Aside from my contact information, my work experience is the most important element of my resume.

A resume makes me look professional and stand out from the other applicants for a job.

It is important not to say anything about what my duties were at a particular employer.

Most of the time, a resume won't really be necessary.

A resume helps me focus on what makes me valuable to an employer.
