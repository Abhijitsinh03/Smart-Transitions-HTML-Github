Building my Smart Transitions Mentor Team

Will it be hard to ask people?

Probably not as hard as you think. When it comes to the invitation part of the task we will give you tips and coach you on what to say. And, while it’s always good to do the asking in person, the actual invitation connecting them to your chat group on your dashboard will be done by an email that will give them plenty of information. Being asked to be a mentor may be new to the people you are approaching but it is also an honor. Most adults, most people, get a positive feeling from helping others, so you are giving them an opportunity to get that positive feeling on a regular basis. If they are unsure, you can simply ask them if you can send them an email invitation and if they do not feel like they can take it on, they can simply decline.
