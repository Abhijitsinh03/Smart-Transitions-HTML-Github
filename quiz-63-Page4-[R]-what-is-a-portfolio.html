What is a Portfolio?

What should I have in my portfolio?

Remember that your portfolio should contain things that back up the claims you will want to make about yourself in an interview. It should support the work brand you have adopted and are promoting. Anything that will technically fit into the 8.5 x 11 inch drop-in pages could work. It should highlight your life experiences your work (paid or volunteer) experiences, reveal something about your passions and interests, attest to your natural talents and skills and reflect your values. The items that might do that include:

  • letters of recommendation from coaches, teachers, employers, or community leaders;
  • certificates and awards;
  • programs from performances or press clippings;
  • photographic evidence attesting to your skills and interests;
  • photographs of artwork, projects, and performances;
  • samples of your ability communicate in writing.

A portfolio is a kind of autobiography. It’s your chance to tell your story with words, images, and documents.
