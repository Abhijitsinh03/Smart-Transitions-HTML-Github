Being Prepared for Men

  • Haircut
  • Shave/beard trim
  • Teeth brushed
  • Piercings (earrings only)
  • Tattoos (cover if possible)
  • Glasses: If you wear them, make sure the lenses are clean.
  • Clothes
    • Good: collared dress shirt.
    • Better: collared dress shirt with tie.
    • Best: collared dress shirt with tie and suit jacket or sports

Being Prepared for Women

  • Hair styled in a natural and relaxed but professional look (note that bright, day-glo colored streaks or colorful hair do not suggest a business professional brand.
  • Make up: If you never wear make-up, don’t worry about make up. If you do wear make-up, simple is often best. Just remember that this isn’t a date. It’s an interview.
    • Avoid: heavy false eyelashes; extreme cat eye eyeliner, smoky eyes, dramatic evening make-up.
  • Glasses: If you wear them, make sure the lenses are clean.
  • Piercings: (earrings only—if you’re really attached to your nose stud, okay, but make sure it fits well against your skin rather than protruding).
    • Tattoos (cover if possible)
  • Clothes
    • Good: simple blouse or dress in neutral color.
    • Better: collared blouse, simple jewelry.
    • Best: above plus business jacket.
    • Avoid: scoop necks; revealing cleavage; bare shoulders.
