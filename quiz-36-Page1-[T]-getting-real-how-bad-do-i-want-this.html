Getting Real How Bad Do I Want This?

Remember back to the Reading “What Do I Need to Be Successful? The four things on that list were a vision (where you wanted to go), a plan (a series of steps to get there), motivation (the will to continue even when things were hard), and a support network (people to turn to for help and advice). You’re almost ready to map out a plan. That’s what turns a dream into a reality. But, before we do that, let’s check back I with your motivation. How motivated are you to pursue this Life Work Vision and career?

There are no right or wrong to the answers below, it just gauges how motivated and excited you are about your Life-Work Vision. It also might suggest some things you could be doing to jumpstart that career right now. You don't have to wait until after high school or college or a skills training program to being pursuing your vision. There are things you can do today to better prepare you for that vision.
