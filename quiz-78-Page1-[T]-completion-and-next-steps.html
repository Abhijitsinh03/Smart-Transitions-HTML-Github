Completion and Next Steps

You should be feeling pretty proud by now. You’ve completed 78 readings and tasks designed to prepare you for the transition to adult life-work.

  • You’ve mastered the steps to success (a vision, a plan, motivation, and a support network)
  • You’ve stacked the D.E.C.K. in your favor by locating documents, recording experiences, assessing critical skills and personal knowledge.
  • You’ve built a support network—your mentor team
  • You’ve gathered critical information about yourself in one location to make it easy to access.
  • You’ve created a resume and references.
  • You’ve begun thinking about potential careers.
  • You’ve become “smart” by knowing yourself (your aspirations, gifts, skills, passions, values, and causes).
  • You’ve created a life-work vision and you have a life-work plan.
  • You’ve been proactive in defining a personal brand and a work brand.
  • You’ve learned the secrets of a successful interview.
  • You understand what employers are looking for and the skills you will need to keep a job, get promoted, and build a career.
  • You’ve built a life-work personal presentation portfolio and learned how to use it.
  • You’ve created a personal web page for marketing yourself digitally

And here you are, ready to take the next step.
