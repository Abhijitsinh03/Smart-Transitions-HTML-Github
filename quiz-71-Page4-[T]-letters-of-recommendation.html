Letters of Recommendation

Method B:

While Method A is a more unified approach to getting letters of recommendation, Method B is simpler. Just think about the relationships you have with special individuals. Who knows you well? Who would be most likely to write you a letter of recommendation? Whose recommendation would be the most impressive (for example, if you are hoping to make a career in healthcare, a doctor or nurse or medical training staff would be impressive)? Just list 4 potential letter writers in the boxes below.

Potential Letter Writer 1B (Example Mr. Smith, Ms. Bailey, Dr. Grant)

Potential Letter Writer 2B (Example Mr. Smith, Ms. Bailey, Dr. Grant)

Potential Letter Writer 3B (Example Mr. Smith, Ms. Bailey, Dr. Grant)

Potential Letter Writer 4B (Example Mr. Smith, Ms. Bailey, Dr. Grant)

When you have at least 4 Letter Writers listed in either Method A or Method B, you can click the PUBLISH Button below. That will create personalized versions of the letter below and locate them in the STO Gallery. From there you can print them out, sign and date them, and mail them or hand deliver them to the potential letter writer.
