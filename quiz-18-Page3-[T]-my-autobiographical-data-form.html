My Autobiographical Data Form

Congratulations!

Congratulations! You have you have earned another 3 points for completing your autobiographical date form. This is a critical form because it is how you update your resume and save important information you will need access to in the employment process.

Now What?

You can do one of several things now.

  1. I you haven’t completed enough of this document, you can take a break exit the course by clicking on the CLOSE Button below. You can return to it when you are ready. You can find your Autobiographical Data Form by clicking on the tab on your dashboard.
  2. If you’ve completed most or all of this form, clicking on “Save My Resume” below will extract data from this form and format it as an attractive resume in PDF format. Your new resume can be found by clicking on the tab on your dashboard. Once you have saved and reviewed your resume you will be asked to share it with your mentor team. Your mentor team will either recommend tweaks and adjustments or approve the Resume. If adjustments are required, return to this document “My Autobiographical Data Form” to make the adjustments and “save” as a new PDF for your mentor team. Once your resume is approved by your team, you can download it and print it out or attach it to an email.
  3. If you’ve completed most or all of this form, but want to wait until you better understand your own personal strengths and skills before saving and creating your resume, that’s fine too. After completing some of the future exercises that focus on personal strengths, you can click on the NEXT READING Button below. You can return to your Autobiographical Data Form by clicking on the tab of your dashboard and editing your answers later.

You are making great progress, but this is one of those tasks that you will need to complete before moving on. If you want to take a break, that’s fine. You can return to this when you are read to continue.
