MY MOTIVATION: Why do I want this?

The strongest motivation can usually be found when the things you are good at doing and the things you love to do and your values intersect with some need the world has. In the examples below, the world needs artists to entertain and excite us, health care professionals to heal us, and skilled trades to build and repair things.

Examples:
  • I love video games and my head is full of ideas about making them better. I can’t imagine a better career than making a living doing something I love.
  • I’ve always wanted to help people, especially children and my aunt was a nurse and she was very well respected.
  • I like working with my hands and building things. I also like being outdoors and not stuck in an office. I want to make a good living to support my family.
