Organizing Your Portfolio

A College Bound Portfolio Layout

  1. Begin with your cover page on page one and your portrait n page 2 (the back side of page one) Then your resumé and references.
  2. Follow with your grades and a list of the classes you have taken.
  3. Next, you will want your college admission test scores (ACTs, SATs).
  4. Select one of your Who I Am pages (put your stronger academic abilities first). Use that page to introduce a section focusing on the ability you have identified. Draw more examples from your collection of portfolio evidence. Include relevant awards and certificates, programs, press clippings, and original artwork.
  5. Repeat step 4 with more of your Who I Am pages.
  6. Add your letters of recommendation.
  7. If you did not put awards and certificates in an earlier section, add them now.
  8. Close with optional lists.
