Ways to Serve

Your Life-Work Vision should reflect some ideas of how you would like to serve the world. We often don’t think about this or are not taught to consider this when considering careers. To help you get into this mindset, we’ve provided a list of general career fields and how they serve the world. If you’ve narrowed down your list of possible career fields to a handful, you can use some of the samples below to build your Life-Work Vision statement.

All you need to do is put a check in the box next to each statement that is relevant for you (up to 8—if you are drawn to more than 8, you probably need to narrow your focus a bit more) and that text will be automatically saved to a PDF document titled “My Ways to Serve” that you can access and refer to as you are creating your Life-Work Vision in the next task.
