Building a Transition Team

STEP TWO:

Now you are going to share your plan with other people who can help you with key aspects of that plan. When you click the READY button below it will generate a custom sheet from the choices you make below that you can print out and take to the people you’ve identified that you want to share your plan with. You’re going to make the call from the selections below of who you want to share your plan with. You will take your three-page plan and review it with them and ask them to initial that they’ve seen it.

Remember that if you click the check box next to the type of support contact, it will be added to your custom transition team list. Not all of the types will apply to you but the more people you share a plan with, the stronger a support network you are building.
