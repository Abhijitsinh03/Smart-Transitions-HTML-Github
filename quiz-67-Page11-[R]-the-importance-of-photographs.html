The Importance of Photographs

Printing Photos

Creating good photos is one thing. Getting them into a print portfolio is another. Here are your options:

  1. Use a service to order prints. Google “photographic prints from my phone” to find some of these services. They will tell you what you need to do, but it usually involves uploading your phone photos and deciding on the size of the print.
  2. Download your photos to a computer and print them from there. Most inkjet printers today can do a decent job of making prints from your digital images if you remember these tips:
    a. Save or export your photos in the largest size possible (or as original). This will allow you to make bigger images that are still sharp and not pixelated.
    b. Invest in glossy photo paper from an office supply store. It isn’t expensive and it really makes your images look nice. If you print a 5 x 7 size horizontal image on a sheet of 8.5 x 11 photopaper, it will fit nicely into your portfolio.
    c. Before you print, make sure your printer dialog box is set for glossy paper and “photo” or the highest quality possible.
