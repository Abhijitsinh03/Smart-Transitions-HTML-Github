Interviewing With Your Portfolio

After the interview:

Until you are really comfortable with the interview process, consider coming back to this reading and checklist to prepare for your next interview.

Identify the following statements as true or false based on the reading above:

  1. If you have a portfolio, you can just hand it over to the interviewer and you won't have to say anything.

  2. before your interview, check that your resume is up to date and print out an extra copy to leave behind.

  3. Use your portfolio to back up your answer to questions or claims about yourself.

  4. Be a little early for an interview 10-15 minutes is good.

  5. Plan to walk someone slowly through every single page in your portfolio.

  6. After the interview, there is nothing you need to do.
