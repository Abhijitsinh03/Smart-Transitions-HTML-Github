What is a Portfolio?

What can a portfolio do for you?

  • Figure out what you want to do!

    • A portfolio can help you recognize and understand your strengths and talents.

  • Get into special training programs!

    • Portfolios can document your special abilities and background.

  • Get into college!

    • Your portfolio can help you to collect and organize the information and documents you will need to apply to college. A nice portfolio can also help during interviews.

  • Find a job!

    • A portfolio can document basic skills and convince a prospective employer that he or she is a perfect fit for a particular job opening. It can also boost your confidence at a job interview. Everyone makes claims about what they can and cannot do, with your portfolio, you have proof.

  • Find a better job!

    • A portfolio can give you an edge when applying for a better job. When competition is stiff, an attractive portfolio can help you make a good impression.

That last reason finding a better job is also something that you may always be interested in doing so it is important to realize that your portfolio is not just something you keep at the beginning of a career, but, like a resume, it is something you continue to update with more recent examples and experiences.
