Method A:

Think about the “Who I Am” header sheets you created earlier representing some of your best qualities, traits and characteristics. In each of the boxes below, list one those qualities, traits, or characteristics and then think of a person who might be able to testify to that claim through writing a letter for you. This is probably the stronger of the two methods because it makes your letters relate directly to things you are trying to promote. There are spaces for 10 in case you had that many “Who I Am” header sheets, but you only need to fill in the info for 4 in order to move on to the next task.
