My Aspirations

Aspirations are the things we aspire to or the things we want out of life. Another way to look at this is to ask how you would know you had been successful. Success has internal definitions, but it usually also comes with material evidence in the kind of lifestyle we have. No one lifestyle is better than others when it comes to success. It’s up to what you want. A mansion is no better than a tent and luxury sports car is no better than a bicycle. It all depends on how you define success for you. For some people, doing a job they love is more of an indicator of success than material trappings. Some people may define success as having a loving family and being able to support and care for them. Others might want material success in the form of visible status symbols. but it does help to be clear about what would indicate success for you.
