Organizing Your Portfolio

Optional lists and presentations you might include

Relax! These are optional.

  • If you read a lot and you think that what you read helps to define you, a reading record might work in your portfolio. You might also consider a listening or viewing record for music or films that have shaped who you are.
  • If you have traveled widely, you might want to list your travels.
  • If you are socially very active and you belong to many organizations, consider listing your club memberships. Include the name of the club, its purpose and your participation in it. If you are or were an officer, don't forget to note it.
