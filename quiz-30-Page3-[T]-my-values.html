My Values

My Values Part 1:

Below is a list of things that are potential core values. Select the 6 values that you think most strongly match who you are at your core by checking the box beside it. This exercise will only allow you to select 6 so you will have to choose carefully. If you have chosen 6 and discover something that you think is more truly a value for you, go back and uncheck one of the other boxes to make room. If you don't see what you consider to be one of your core values, write it in in the text box at the end of the list. Take your time with this exercise.
