My Personal Brand

Your personal brand is or should be who you really, honestly are when you are relaxed with friends and family, but it is the best version of you. We all have bad habits or ways of behaving that sometimes don't serve us well, but the personal brand we adopt, cultivate, communicate, and defend is the self we aspire to be. Your personal brand will be the basis for your work brand but is a bit more general and not so focused on who we are at work.
